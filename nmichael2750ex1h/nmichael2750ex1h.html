Description:

Nate has 4 rambunctious children and he's terrible with managing his finances. He would like the aid of a program to help track weekly allowances so his kids quit pestering him about it. The software would maintain individual kid's current balance, chores and/or expected tasks with completion tracking, as well as record when and where allowance funds have been spent for each child. It should have different options based on type of user (parent - full control, vs. child - read only).  Finally, the program should be easily accessible on a variety of different devices/platforms (iOS, Android, Web).

Use Cases:

  • As a parent, I want to add/edit/remove a child
  • As a parent, I want to A/E/R an allowance
  • As a parent, I want to A/E/R a chore
  • As a parent, I want to add an expenditure
  • As a parent, I want to get a list of balances
  • As a parent, I want to get a list of expenditures
  • As a parent, I want to get a list of uncompleted chores
